Spotify MashUp Playlist Generator

Instructions

To get started, please follow the steps below:

  1. Enter the names of your favorite artists in the provided fields. Please note that you can enter up to five artists and each artist's name should be less than 50 characters long and should not contain any commas.
  2. Next, enter a name for your playlist in the provided field. The playlist name should also be less than 50 characters long.
  3. Click on the 'Login & Generate Playlist' button to generate your playlist.

What happens next?

Once you click on the 'Login & Generate Playlist' button, our system will create a playlist based on your liked artists' tracks you have entered.
